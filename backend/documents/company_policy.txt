STORE POLICIES AND TERMS

1. PRODUCT INFORMATION
- Product names, prices, stock status, images, and product URLs are sourced from the Magento storefront catalog.
- Catalog availability and pricing can change without prior notice.

2. SEARCH EXPERIENCE
- The chat assistant currently supports keyword search, price filtering, product detail lookup, and paginated result browsing.
- Additional filters may be added later as the storefront integration expands.

3. PRODUCT LINKS
- "View Product" actions open the relevant product page on the Magento storefront.
- Final product information should be confirmed on the storefront product page.

4. RETURNS AND EXCHANGES
- This demo assistant does not process returns or exchanges directly.
- Return and exchange decisions should follow the storefront's published policies.

5. PAYMENTS AND ORDERS
- The chat assistant does not place orders or process payments.
- Checkout, payment, and order confirmation happen on the storefront website.

6. CUSTOMER SUPPORT
- The assistant helps with discovery and product lookup.
- If a request requires account access, order history, or checkout support, the user should continue on the storefront.

7. PRIVACY
- The chat assistant should only use information needed to answer the current request.
- Sensitive account or payment information should not be requested in chat.
