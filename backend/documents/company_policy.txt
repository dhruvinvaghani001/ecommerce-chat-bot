REAL ESTATE COMPANY - POLICIES AND TERMS

1. PROPERTY VIEWING POLICY
- All property viewings must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance.
- Clients must present valid identification before entering any property.
- A maximum of 3 viewings per day can be arranged per client.
- Virtual tours are available for all listed properties upon request.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
- A booking deposit of 10% is required to reserve any property.
- Payment plans are available for off-plan properties with flexible installments.
- For ready properties, full payment or mortgage approval is required within 30 days of signing the MOU.
- Service charges are paid annually and vary by community.
- All prices listed are in AED (United Arab Emirates Dirham).

3. REFUND POLICY
- Booking deposits are refundable within 14 days if the buyer cancels before signing the Sale and Purchase Agreement (SPA).
- After SPA signing, a cancellation fee of 25% of the property value applies.
- Processing time for refunds is 30-45 business days.

4. AGENT COMMISSION
- The standard agent commission is 2% of the property sale price.
- Commission is paid upon completion of the transaction.
- Rental commissions are 5% of the annual rent.

5. PROPERTY MANAGEMENT SERVICES
- We offer full property management services for landlords.
- Monthly management fee: 5% of monthly rent collected.
- Services include tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and regular property inspections.

6. WARRANTY AND MAINTENANCE
- New properties come with a 1-year defects liability period from the developer.
- Structural warranty is 10 years as per UAE law.
- We assist in filing and following up on any warranty claims with the developer.

7. DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS
For buyers:
- Valid passport copy
- UAE visa/Emirates ID (for residents)
- Proof of income / bank statements
- NOC from developer (for resale properties)

For renters:
- Valid passport copy
- UAE visa/Emirates ID
- Employment contract or salary certificate
- Security deposit (typically 5% of annual rent for unfurnished, 10% for furnished)

8. CONTACT INFORMATION
- Customer Service: Available 24/7 via chat
- Office Hours: Sunday to Thursday, 9 AM to 6 PM
- Emergency Maintenance: Available 24/7 for managed properties
- Email: support@example-realestate.com
- Phone: +971 4 123 4567

9. PRIVACY POLICY
- All personal information is handled in accordance with UAE data protection laws.
- Client data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent.
- Clients can request deletion of their data at any time.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
- All disputes are first handled through our internal mediation process.
- If unresolved, disputes are escalated to the Dubai Land Department (DLD).
- The governing law is the law of the United Arab Emirates.
