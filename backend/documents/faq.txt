FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

Q: What kind of products are available in the store?
A: The store offers apparel and related catalog items from the Magento demo catalog, including jackets, shorts, tops, and fitness wear.

Q: How can I find products within a budget?
A: You can ask for products under a price, above a price, or between two prices. The assistant currently supports keyword search plus price filtering.

Q: Can I filter by size, color, or category?
A: Not yet in this chat flow. The current assistant supports keyword search and price filters only.

Q: How do I open a product page?
A: Product cards include a "View Product" action that opens the corresponding product page on the Magento storefront.

Q: Can I see more results?
A: Yes. Product result cards include pagination controls so you can move between result pages.

Q: How do I get more details about one product?
A: Use the "Get Details" action on a product card or ask for details by product name.

Q: Are prices shown in the store currency?
A: Yes. Prices are displayed using the currency returned by the Magento catalog API.

Q: What happens if a product is out of stock?
A: The widget shows the stock badge returned by the catalog so you can tell whether an item is in stock.
