FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

Q: What areas do you cover?
A: We cover all major areas in Dubai including Dubai Marina, JVC (Jumeirah Village Circle), Downtown Dubai, Palm Jumeirah, Arabian Ranches, Al Barsha, Business Bay, and more.

Q: Can foreigners buy property in Dubai?
A: Yes! Foreigners can purchase property in designated freehold areas in Dubai. There are no restrictions on nationality for property purchases in freehold zones.

Q: What is the minimum investment for a Golden Visa through property?
A: As of 2024, you need to invest a minimum of AED 2 million in property to be eligible for a 10-year Golden Visa. The property must be fully paid (no mortgage).

Q: Do you offer mortgage assistance?
A: Yes, we partner with major banks in the UAE to help you secure the best mortgage rates. We can arrange pre-approval before you start property hunting. Typical mortgage rates range from 3.5% to 5.5% per annum.

Q: What is an off-plan property?
A: An off-plan property is one that is purchased before construction is complete. These often come with attractive payment plans (e.g., 60/40 or 70/30 split between construction and handover) and can offer significant price advantages.

Q: How long does the buying process take?
A: Typically 30-60 days from offer acceptance to completion. This includes:
- Offer acceptance and MOU signing: 1-3 days
- Due diligence and NOC: 7-14 days
- Transfer at Dubai Land Department: 1 day
- Key handover: Same day or within 3 days

Q: What are the ongoing costs of property ownership?
A: Ongoing costs include:
- Service charges: AED 10-30 per sqft annually (varies by community)
- DEWA (utilities): Varies by usage
- Insurance: Optional but recommended
- Maintenance: Budget 1-2% of property value annually

Q: Can I rent out my property?
A: Absolutely! Dubai has a thriving rental market. We can manage the entire rental process for you, including:
- Market analysis and pricing
- Professional photography and listing
- Tenant screening and verification
- Lease agreement preparation
- Rent collection and maintenance

Q: What is the RERA fee?
A: RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Agency) charges include:
- Property registration: 4% of purchase price + AED 580
- Mortgage registration: 0.25% of loan amount + AED 290
- Oqood (off-plan): 4% of purchase price

Q: Do you provide after-sale service?
A: Yes, our relationship doesn't end at the sale. We provide:
- Property management services
- Assistance with utility connections (DEWA, internet)
- Interior design referrals
- Maintenance coordination
- Resale assistance when you're ready to sell
